About us

Scoopsville is a small ice cream shop located in the heart of Los Angeles, CA. It was founded in April 2020 by a pair of college roommates. Together, they connected through grabbing a scoop of ice cream every Sunday afternoon.

Now, they strive to bring Angelenos together through their delicious frozen creations, one scoop at a time.
