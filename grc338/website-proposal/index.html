Introduction

Scoopsville

Scoopsville is a small ice cream shop located in the heart of Los Angeles, CA. It was founded in April 2020 by a pair of college roommates. Together, they connected through grabbing a scoop of ice cream every Sunday afternoon. Now, they strive to bring Angelenos together through their delicious frozen creations, one scoop at a time.

Target audience

Our target audience consists of individuals who share a love for ice cream. These are people looking to try new flavors, trying to find their favorite ice cream shop, or people who simply want a delicious frozen treat to enjoy.

Our target audience will visit our website to view our ice cream flavors and their ingredients, learn more about us, view our location and regular store hours, and contact us for any questions, concerns, or special requests.
